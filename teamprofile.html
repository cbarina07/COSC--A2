How We Learn

From the test results, a large proportion of our group are visual learners, I.e they learn by reading or seeing pictures. This means that statistically, the majority of our group will struggle with spoken direction as they are easily distracted by noise.

Considering this, it would be beneficial to meet in quiet places, not in libraries or cafeterias, and to either use a white board, share someone's screen if meeting online, or by turning on all video cameras to benefit the majority of the group.

Also creating a meeting notes list at the end so that the group may refer to it later would be ideal.
